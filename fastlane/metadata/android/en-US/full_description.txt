Orca connects to an Orca server that you operate.

Share or paste a media URL, monitor server-side downloads, search history, manage per-site cookies and resolution preferences, and play or share completed media.

The Android app is a thin client. Downloads run on your own server through yt-dlp. Orca has no ads, analytics, tracking SDK, or hosted account service.

Use Orca only for media you are authorized to access and download.
